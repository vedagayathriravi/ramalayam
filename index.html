About the Temple

A Sacred Abode of Sri Rama

Established for the spiritual upliftment of the Ramnagar community, our Ramalayam has been a beacon of devotion, dharma, and seva for generations of devotees in Ongole.

Heritage

Built and nurtured by the devoted residents of Ramnagar, our temple stands as a testimony to faith, unity, and the timeless bond between guru and devotee.

Deities

The sanctum houses the divine forms of Sri Rama, Mata Sita, Lakshmana Swamy, and Sri Anjaneya Swamy — radiant in eternal grace.

Seva & Community

Beyond worship, the temple is the heart of Annadanam, Vedic learning, bhajan sandhyas, and youth programs that keep our culture vibrant and alive.

“रामो विग्रहवान् धर्मः साधुः सत्यपराक्रमः ।
राजा सर्वस्य लोकस्य देवानामिव वासवः ॥”

“Rama is the embodiment of dharma — noble, truthful, and valiant; the king of all the worlds, like Indra among the gods.”

Daily Schedule

Darshan & Aarti Timings

The temple welcomes devotees throughout the day. Plan your visit around the sacred rituals to experience the temple at its most divine.

05:30 AM

Suprabhatam

Awakening of the Lord with sacred verses and the gentle ringing of bells.

06:00 AM

Abhishekam

Ritual bathing of the deities with milk, curd, honey, and pure water.

07:30 AM

Morning Aarti

Offering of camphor flame accompanied by Vedic chants and devotional bhajans.

12:00 PM

Madhyahna Naivedyam

Mid-day offering followed by temple closure for the Lord's rest.

04:00 PM

Sayam Darshan

Evening reopening — a peaceful time for personal prayer and sankalpa.

07:00 PM

Sandhya Aarti

The most auspicious aarti of the day, illuminating the sanctum with divine light.

Note: Timings may vary on festival days, ekadashi, and special tithis. Please check the announcements section or call the temple office before visiting.

Sacred Calendar

Festivals & Celebrations

Throughout the year, the temple comes alive with vibrant festivals, processions, and community gatherings — each a celebration of our timeless traditions.

Chaitra

Sri Rama Navami

The grand birthday of Lord Rama. Nine days of pravachanam, kalyanotsavam, and a magnificent rathotsavam through the streets of Ramnagar.

Vaishakha

Hanuman Jayanti

Special abhishekam, sundara kanda parayanam, and distribution of vada-mala prasadam to devotees.

Sravana

Varalakshmi Vratam

Auspicious pooja for married women, with collective sankalpam in the temple prakaram.

Aswayuja

Dussehra & Vijayadasami

Ten nights of devi puja, Ramayana kathakalakshepam, and the symbolic burning of Ravana on the temple grounds.

Karthika

Karthika Deepotsavam

The temple is illuminated with thousands of oil lamps; deepa-aradhana every evening of the sacred month.

Pushya

Vaikunta Ekadasi

Devotees pass through the Uttara Dwaram for blessings of moksha, followed by annadanam.
